Weapon Calculator

For calculating weapon related stuff such as momentum, penetration and similar.

How to use:

There are two ways of using this: You either use vanilla weapons/attacks/materials/races by selecting them in the drop down. Or you introduce the details by hand.

Back

Attacker characteristics

This dropdown is to load the sizes and characteristics of the average vanilla races. If you want to try a modded race you can specify their stats in there.






Weapon characteristics

This dropdown is to load the values of the vanilla weapons. Bellow, you can write specific characteristics in case the weapon is not on the list, or you want to create a weapon yourself.








Weapon material

This dropdown is to load the values of the vanilla material. Bellow, you can write specific characteristics in case the material is not on the list, or you want to create a material yourself.








Armor layers

To specify various armor layers and their characteristics.

The first layer is the one on the outside and each layer you add after it is considered to be under the previous layers.
